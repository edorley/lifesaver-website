About Us

Building Financial
Futures in Liberia

Lifesaver Micro Lending Business Inc. is a registered microfinance institution dedicated to expanding access to affordable financial services for individuals, micro-entrepreneurs, and small businesses across Liberia.

Operating in line with the regulatory framework of the Central Bank of Liberia, we are committed to transparency, ethical lending, and financial inclusion. Our services are tailored to meet the needs of underserved communities while empowering clients to build resilience, grow their businesses, and improve their quality of life.

Our Services

Financial Solutions
Tailored for You

We offer a range of micro-credit solutions designed to support income-generating activities and strengthen livelihoods.

Personal Loans

Individual loans tailored to meet your personal financial needs with flexible repayment terms and competitive rates.

Employee / Salary Loans

Quick access to credit for salaried employees. Convenient repayment through salary deductions with fast approval.

Small Business Loans

Working capital and growth financing for small businesses looking to expand operations and increase revenue.

Entrepreneur Loans

Startup and growth capital for micro-entrepreneurs with innovative ideas and the drive to succeed.

Asset Acquisition Loans

Finance the purchase of essential assets — vehicles, equipment, machinery — to help grow your business capacity.

School Fees Loans

Invest in education with our school fees financing. Ensure uninterrupted learning for your children and dependents.
